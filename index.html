03 About

We don't sell 0days.

ZeroSploit is an independent vulnerability research group. We pick targets based on real-world deployment — software that organizations depend on — and audit until we find what the vendor missed.

Every finding becomes a complete technical writeup. How we found it, how we exploited it, what defenders can do. No teaser posts. No paywalled details.

We believe defenders and attackers should not have asymmetric access to information. So we publish everything.
